Welcome to Trafford Sword Club

Trafford Sword Club is a fencing institution that teaches the sophisticated Olympic sport of fencing. The club caters to to two distinct age groups, namely juniors between the ages of 8 and 16 and adults aged 16 and above. The club teaches fencing techniques across all three blades, namely foil, epee, and sabre. The club prides itself on its ability to cater to fencers of all skill levels, ranging from complete beginners to advanced fencers.

Whether you are seeking to hone your existing skills or embark on a journey towards mastering the art of fencing, the Trafford Sword Club is the perfect place for you.
