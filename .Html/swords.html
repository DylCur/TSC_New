Swords

Foil: A light, thin weapon that is used for thrusting attacks to the torso. Points are scored by touching the opponent with the tip of the foil. The target area is limited to the torso, including the back but not the arms or legs.

Epee: A heavier weapon with a stiffer blade that is used for thrusting attacks to the entire body. Points are scored by touching the opponent with the tip of the epee. The target area is the entire body, including the head and limbs.

Sabre: A weapon with a flat blade and a curved guard that is used for both thrusting and cutting attacks to the head, torso, and arms. Points are scored by touching the opponent with the edge or tip of the sabre, and the target area includes the head, torso, and arms. The blade is also allowed to hit the opponent with the flat edge, as long as the hit is above the waist.
